Marry Christmas

TO ALL!

Unwrap the moments of bonhomie with merry-making. The enchantment of Christmas lies in the celebration of the birth of Jesus Christ.
Every year Christmas falls on 25th December bringing lots of prosperity and blessings. It is a season to wish one another good health, fortune, and abundance.
Children eagerly wait on this day to receive presents from Santa.

Christmas is here and you can literally smell the festive cheer in the air. Every year, Christmas is celebrated on December 25. The day is celebrated as the birth anniversary of Jesus Christ. People attend Christmas Mass at midnight, visit the church, cook up decadent feasts for their loved ones, bake cookies and plum cakes, decorate their homes with mistletoes and twinkling lights, hang stockings, set up Christmas trees adorned with ornaments, hang wreaths on their front doors, and exchange gifts. It is a joyous day with many traditions.


Best wishes for a joyous Christmas filled with love, happiness and prosperity!
